Extra help for this step

No extra help on this step.

Make sure they're far enough apart so when you're going left and right you don't make contact with the other triggers.

Make "Start Active" is disabled at the top right of the animate menu.

To go into a group, do right thumbstick up and press. To go out of a group, do right thumbstick down and press.

If you're making it go up and down, place the block far enough away, so it cant be seen poking through anywhere.

If it's barely working at the end, increase the time it takes to travel to the bottom.

Make sure the first and last triggers in the line are below the block that moves down in the animation.

Make "Start Active" is enabled at the top right of the animate menu.
